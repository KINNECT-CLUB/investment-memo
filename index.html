Who We Serve
The Family Connector — In every modern family, one person carries the weight of connection. They remember the anniversaries. They call grandma. They're quietly terrified that the stories, the documents, the voices will disappear before anyone thinks to capture them. They're 30–55, and they show up in every kind of family — first-generation, multicultural, blended, geographically scattered. In any couple, one of the two is already thinking about this. Kinnect is built for that person — and through them, for the whole family.
Secondary: Anyone who has just experienced a loss or near-loss — a diagnosis, a death, a friend gone too soon — and feels the urgent need to capture what's left before more disappears.
Investment Thesis
Every competitor solves the Head — wills, documents, estate tools. Nobody solves the Heart — stories, voices, the feeling of being remembered. Kinnect solves both. The bigger opportunity is what sits underneath: Octopus, Kinnect's AI family intelligence layer, is positioned to become the trusted connector across the family data ecosystem — not another silo adding to the fragmentation families already face.

As Kinnect accumulates voice data, relationship graphs, and document history (with explicit consent), it becomes the intermediary that understands a family's full picture and surfaces what they need across products, partners, and life stages — like an operating system for family legacy, private, permissioned, and compounding daily. With 1,300+ members, 84 paying families, 490 Storybooks, 4 signed B2B partnerships — all built with $480K in under two years — this is no longer a thesis. It's an early business with a moat that compounds every day.

The Kinnect Platform

No ads. No addictive algorithms. No data sales. A private, invite-only platform where families connect, record stories, and save generational documents — across iOS, Android, and web. Not an app. A platform and a brand — built with partners who share the belief that technology should bring families closer, not fragment them further.

  • Family Tree — Most inclusive tree on the market. 30 nodes per member. Every node is a potential subscriber and partner referral. AI surfaces what's missing: "Your daughter doesn't have health insurance on file."
  • Vault — Secure family document storage. If a document is missing, Kinnect connects the family to a trusted partner (FastWill, Gentreo). Revenue for Kinnect. Value for the family.
  • Octopus (AI Layer) — Family intelligence engine. Learns relationships, patterns, and gaps. As voice data and family graphs accumulate, Octopus becomes the trusted connector — reducing fragmentation rather than adding to it.
  • Daily Echo + Nudge — AI-personalized daily prompts and monthly relationship investment. 490 Storybooks, 279 voice recordings, 80% onboarding completion.
  • Printed StoryBook Export — High-margin physical artifact with QR codes linking to multimedia. The most durable Kinnect product: a book that exists in the world.
