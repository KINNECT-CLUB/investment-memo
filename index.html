Who We Serve
The Family Connector — In every modern family, one person carries the weight of connection. They remember the anniversaries. They call grandma. They're quietly terrified that the stories, the documents, the voices will disappear before anyone thinks to capture them. They show up in every kind of family — first-generation, multicultural, blended, geographically scattered. In any couple, one of the two is already thinking about this. Kinnect is built for that person — and through them, for the whole family.

The trigger is almost always the same: a diagnosis, a death, or a quiet fear that no one else will say out loud.
Investment Thesis
Every competitor solves the Head — wills, documents, estate tools. Nobody solves the Heart — stories, voices, the feeling of being remembered. Kinnect solves both. Underneath the consumer product sits Octopus — an AI layer that maps a family's relationships, detects document gaps, and routes them to the right partner at the right moment. Private by design. Revenue-generating from day one. And the longer it runs, the less any competitor can touch it.

Nine months ago, Omar rebuilt the team around a sharper vision. Three platforms, six signed partnerships, a working AI infrastructure, 1,300 members — built with no paid ads and $480K. This round isn't a bet on potential. It's a bet on a team that already proved it, and is ready to stop holding back.

The Kinnect Platform
Family Tree
The most inclusive tree on the market — biological, blended, chosen. AI surfaces what's missing: "Your daughter doesn't have health insurance on file." Every node is a potential subscriber and partner referral.
Vault
Secure family document storage. Missing documents connect families to trusted partners (FastWill, Gentreo). Revenue for Kinnect. Value for the family.
Octopus — AI Layer
Family intelligence engine. Learns relationships, patterns, and gaps. Routes families to the right partner at the right moment — estate planning, senior care, document gaps. Revenue-generating from day one. No competitor can replicate what Octopus has already learned about a family.
Daily Echo + Nudge
AI-personalized daily prompts and monthly relationship investment. The content families create accumulates over time into something no other platform has — and cannot be taken anywhere else.
Printed StoryBook Export
High-margin physical artifact with QR codes linking to multimedia. A book that exists in the world, outside any platform.

Go-to-Market

Direct (B2C): Omar's brand background drives organic content. Zero paid acquisition. Initial wedge: bereaved individuals, caregivers, families navigating illness — high-urgency, high word-of-mouth.

B2B2C: Partners send families at their highest-urgency moments — senior living move-in, will creation, a new child. Our platform keeps them. Near-zero CAC from partner inbound.

B2B: Senior living at $2K–$5K/community/yr. Will/estate referral revenue. Brand partnership deals. Employee benefits licensing.

Future: Printed StoryBook export. Octopus partner API. No advertising. Ever.
